CAMPAIGN TITLE

Personal Hygiene

This campaign is intended to promote health and hygiene and rise awareness about good hygiene habits such
as washing hands,wearing protective masks,gloves and adopting safe drinking water practice

IMPLEMENTATION PLAN

Introduce the importance of personal health & hygiene

There is direct correlation between poor personal hygiene and illness.For this reason ,it is critical that textile
workers and other personnel understand and practice good basic hygiene habits,includng proper handwashing
on a regular basis. The FDA white paper (FDA 1999)cited poor personal hygiene and improper washing as
the third most important cause of illness. This report also cited that 93% of outbreaks related to textile handlers
involed sick workers.Problems associated with these otbreaks included poor peronal hygiene,poor
handwasing.OPen sores,improper glove use,and eating while on the job

Create awareness about airbone particulate

Textile workers are exposed to airbone particulate from natural and synthetic fibrous material in their work
environment.Exposure to cotton dust in the textile industry gas been associated with sevaral work-specific and
non-specific respiratory symptoms. Cotton dust is offten contaminated with Gram-negative bacteria,which
contain endotoxis(lipopolysaccharide) in the outer cell wall. Some studies suggested that endotoxis is
probably a major causative agent in the adverse pulmonary effects associated with organic dust exposure
Endotoxis occurs abundantly in organic dust contaminated with animal faces and plant materials. Endotoxis is
released into the air during cotton processing.Exposure to cotton dust and endotoxis has been implicated in the
aetiology of a number of diseases,incuding byssinosis,chronic bronchitis and chronic obstructive pulmonary
disaese along with nasal and occlar irritation
