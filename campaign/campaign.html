CAMPAIGN TITLE

Personal Hygiene

SHORT DESCRIPTION

This campaign is intended to promote health and hygiene and rise awareness about good hygiene habits such
as washing hands,wearing protective masks,gloves and adopting safe drinking water practice

IMPLEMENTATION PLAN

Introduce the importance of personal health & hygiene

There is direct correlation between poor personal hygiene and illness.For this reason ,it is critical that textile
workers and other personnel understand and practice good basic hygiene habits,includng proper handwashing
on a regular basis. The FDA white paper(FDA 1999)cited poor personal hygiene and improper washing as
the third most important cause of illness. This report also cited that 93% of outbreaks related to textile handlers
involed sick workers.Problems associated with these otbreaks included poor peronal hygiene,poor
handwasing.OPen sores,improper glove use,and eating while on the job
